# Naver Blog & Search Marketing (네이버 마케팅)

## Overview
Naver is **not Google** — it ranks its own ecosystem (블로그·카페·플레이스·쇼핑) above the open web, and ranking is driven by **C-Rank (source trust) + D.I.A+ (document quality/intent)**, not backlinks. Consistent, topically-focused, genuinely-useful posting from an established blog beats keyword-stuffing. 어뷰징(spam tricks) gets you 저품질(quality penalty) that is very hard to recover from.

## When to Use
- 네이버 블로그/포스트 상위노출, 스마트스토어 SEO, 플레이스 최적화
- 애드포스트 monetization (pairs with `publisher-ad-revenue`)
- 네이버 키워드 광고(파워링크), 검색 노출 전략
- Any Korean-market content where Naver is the primary discovery channel

## Naver ranking factors (vs Google)
| | Naver | Google |
|--|-------|--------|
| Core signal | C-Rank (blog/source authority) + D.I.A+ (doc quality) | Links + content + E-E-A-T |
| Favors | 블로그·카페·플레이스·쇼핑 (own properties) | Open web |
| Freshness | High weight, especially local/트렌드 | Moderate |
| Backlinks | Weak signal | Strong signal |
| Kills you | 어뷰징 → 저품질 블로그 | Spam → manual action |

**C-Rank** = how much Naver trusts *this blog* on *this topic* (built by consistent focused posting over time).
**D.I.A+** = how good *this specific post* is (원본성, 정보 충실도, 체류시간, intent match).

## Posting that ranks
- **Topical focus:** one blog, a consistent 주제군 → builds C-Rank. Scattered topics dilute it.
- **Keyword in title + naturally in body**, but write for humans; 키워드 반복 남발 = 저품질 신호.
- **Real substance:** original photos, first-hand info, structure, enough length. 체류시간(dwell time) matters.
- **Consistency:** steady cadence > burst then silence.
- **Keyword research:** 네이버 키워드도구, 블랙키위, 검색량 vs 문서량(경쟁도) — target where demand > supply.

## Channels
- **블로그**: content/SEO + 애드포스트 수익.
- **스마트스토어**: product SEO (상품명 키워드, 카테고리, 리뷰); 쇼핑검색 랭킹.
- **플레이스**: local business (방문자리뷰, 예약, 사진) — critical for offline.
- **파워링크/키워드광고**: paid CPC on search — for immediate traffic vs organic.

## Common Mistakes
- Applying Google-SEO backlink tactics → irrelevant on Naver.
- 어뷰징 (매크로, 키워드 반복, 서로이웃 품앗이, 복붙) → 저품질 penalty, near-unrecoverable.
- New blog expecting instant 상위노출 → C-Rank needs time; no shortcut.
- Ignoring 체류시간/원본성 → thin posts don't rank even with right keywords.
- Mixing unrelated topics on one blog → weak C-Rank everywhere.
- Copy-pasted product descriptions on 스마트스토어 → no 원본성, buried in 쇼핑검색.
